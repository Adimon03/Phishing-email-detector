## Common Phishing Techniques Demonstrated

### 1. **Urgency and Threats**
All samples use time pressure to force quick decisions without thinking:
- "24 hours", "48 hours", "2 hours"
- "Act now or lose access"
- "Immediate action required"

### 2. **Suspicious Domains**
Multiple red flags in email addresses:
- Wrong TLDs (.tk, .ml, .ga, .cf, .xyz, .top)
- Hyphenated domains (paypal-security, chase-bank-verify)
- Extra words in domain (netflix-update, linkedin-jobs)

### 3. **Requests for Sensitive Information**
All samples ask for information legitimate companies never request via email:
- Social Security Numbers
- Credit card numbers and CVV
- Bank account details
- Passwords and PINs
- Driver's license numbers

### 4. **Too Good to Be True Offers**
- Unexpected prizes ($5,000 gift card)
- Large refunds ($3,127)
- High-paying jobs ($8,500/month)
- Free money or rewards

### 5. **Fear Tactics**
- Account suspension threats
- Virus warnings
- Unauthorized transactions
- Identity theft warnings
- Account deletion threats

### 6. **Impersonation**
Pretending to be trusted entities:
- PayPal, Amazon, Microsoft
- Banks (Chase)
- Government (IRS)
- Streaming services (Netflix)
- Professional networks (LinkedIn)
- Shipping companies (FedEx)
